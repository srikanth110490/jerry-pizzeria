Welcome to Jerry’s Pizzeria. We are located in the Eastern Townships in the College/University town of Lennoxville.We have been here for a long time (Est.1973), we plan on staying another 48 years to serve all the hungry patrons that love our food. The secret is in our sauce, pizza sauce that is. We specialize in pizza, the definition of a pizza says: Pizza is a traditional Italian dish consisting of a yeasted flatbread typically topped with tomato sauce and cheese and baked in a stone oven. It can also be topped with additional vegetables, meats, and condiments, and can be made without cheese We try and honor that definition,but you need to come and be your own judge. We don’t think you will be disappointed.
